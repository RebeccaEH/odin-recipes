Strawberry Breakfast Smoothie

Description

Oatmeal based smoothie with a deep pink colour and rich creamy texture

Filling and perfect for a quick breakfast when you have a busy morning

Ingredients

  • 250ml Milk (Dairy or Plant Based)
  • 100g Oats
  • 14 Frozen Strawberries
  • 1 Banana, Broken into chunks
  • 1.5tsp Sugar (Optional)
  • 0.5tsp Vanilla Extract (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Blend milk, oats, strawberries and banana together until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract if desired and continue to blend until smooth
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
