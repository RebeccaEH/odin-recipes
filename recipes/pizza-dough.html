Pizza Dough

Description

Quick pizza dough with a few ingredients - no need to wait for it to rise!

Ingredients

  • 125g All Purpose Flour
  • 1tbsp White Sugar
  • 7g Packet of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 236ml Water
  • 2tbsp Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Combine flour, sugar, yeast and salt in a large bowl. Mix in water and oil until dough comes together
  3. Preheat oven to 190c
  4. Pat and stretch dough onto a baking tray
  5. Cover with pizza sauce and toppings
  6. Cook for 20 to 25 mins
